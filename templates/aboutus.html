About Us

Your Personalized Weather Experience

Welcome to WeatherPredict

At WeatherPredict, we blend the latest in meteorological data with cutting-edge machine learning to forecast not just the weather, but how much you'll enjoy it.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide accurate and personalized weather predictions that help you plan your day with confidence. Whether you're heading to work, planning a picnic, or just deciding what to wear, WeatherPredict is here to ensure you know exactly what to expect from the weather and how it might impact your mood and activities.

How It Works

WeatherPredict leverages advanced data analytics and machine learning to forecast the probability of you enjoying the weather on any given day. Here's how we do it:

  • Data Collection: We gather real-time weather data from reliable sources like Open-Meteo, including temperature, cloud cover, precipitation, wind speed, and humidity.
  • User Feedback: By analyzing historical data and user feedback, we understand how different weather conditions affect people's moods and activities.
  • Machine Learning: Our sophisticated Random Forest Classifier model processes this data to predict the likelihood of good or bad weather experiences for you, based on your personal preferences and past feedback.
  • Personalized Forecasts: We present you with a detailed weather forecast, along with the probability of you having a good or bad day based on the weather conditions.
